Scientists Give Mice Night Vision

Scientists have developed a genetic therapy that can give mice night vision. This is a major breakthrough that could have implications for the treatment of human blindness.

Geothermal Energy Could Heat Homes

Geothermal energy is a renewable energy source that could be used to heat homes and reduce our dependence on fossil fuels. However, there are still challenges that need to be overcome before geothermal energy can be widely adopted.

5G-STARDUST: Unleashing the Potential of 5G from the Skies

A new technology called 5G-STARDUST (Skyborne Transparent Radio Access for Duplex Uplink and Downlink Services) aims to expand the reach and capabilities of 5G networks by deploying them from balloons and drones. This could revolutionize the way we access and utilize 5G connectivity.

British Library in Peril: Cyber Attack Could Cost Up to £7 Million

The British Library is facing a potential cyber attack that could cost up to £7 million to remediate. The attack highlights the growing threat of cybercrime and the importance of robust cybersecurity measures.
